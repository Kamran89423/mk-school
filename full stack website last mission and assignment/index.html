Welcome to Mk school

MK International School, Layyah,
English Section, is a non-profit English
medium school, designed primarily to serve
the needs of the children from
ages 3-18 of the Pakistani Layyah Community.
The school was established in September 2024
under the supervision of the AbdulRaheem Abdullah
with Muhammad kamran, the Vice chancellor of Ghazi university, as its
Principal.




Our principal
